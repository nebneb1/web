- Games -

The NANO soundtrack was made with the idea of making one piece that develops throughout the game, while still having a distinct mood/feeling with each part. This was to create a sence of wholeness throughout the work, the implementation is really what realized this idea. I programmed a seemless audio controller that flows between each segment that all ties in with the sfx, creating a complete audio expirence.

"Everything here works, the design, the cinematic mood, the sound effects are on point... the soundtrack that in conception, execution and implementation is impeccable"
- Phelippe Afonso

Cyberdad is a published 2D cyberpunk gunfight dungeon crawler about a scientist who abandoned the company he worked for because of their inhumane practices. After the scientist's boss kidnaps his daughter to try and get him back... he comes back, with a ​vengeance. Me and a team of 7 worked on this for about 2 months. For this project, I was the audio lead, and I was responsible for composing all of the music and designing SFX. I was a major voice when it came to design, but I also helped along the way with minor contributions to the art, programming, and even put together the trailer.
