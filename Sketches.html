Sketches

I have a unique workflow that helps me get inspired and start producing - I do 1-2 hour long projects, quickly getting all the potential moods or feelings that would work for a project written down, and whether the end result its good or bad I keep everything saved. Afterwords I can show these sketches to other people in whatever team I'm working on. I take the ideas and moods that are agreed on and use aspects from all the different ideas to combine them into one solidified work.
